Hello!

The Bayonetta series is an action-adventure video game series developed by Platinum Games. The series began with the game Bayonetta, introduced on the Xbox console on 2009. Later the success of Bayonetta spawned two sequels, Bayonetta 2 in 2014 on the Wii U, and then Bayonetta 3 which came out October 28th of 2022 for the Nintendo Switch!

The Bayonetta series follows the main lead…Bayonetta! Who is a powerful witch that goes different unique missions utilizing her demonic witch powers, high heels, and unique weapons to defeat powerful Angels.

The Bayonetta series is filled with intense action-packed fights, over-the-top destruction, and recognizable characters and strong stories to reel the player in to a mesmerizing gaming experience.
